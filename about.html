About

The Law, Public and Safety program is to prepare their students to be a officer , security guard, or even a firefighter in their near future. For example, they could even guard a prison or even explain to someone what their rights are.


There are multiple hobbies and activities that the Law, Public and Safety students could get into to understand more about the Law, Public and Safety program.



Such as,


Mock Trial

Work as a page at the legislature

Visit a local courthouse or public safety office

Volunteer with programs for at-risk youth or ex-offenders

Job shadow a lawyer at a legal firm

Attend court hearings and other public legal procedures
